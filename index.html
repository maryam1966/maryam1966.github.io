About Me

Maryam Javed is an accomplished Pakistani digital artist, boasting over two years of experience collaborating with Bahria University Islamabad, and leading creative agencies. Her diverse creativity encompasses 2D/3D design and animation, branding, illustration, logo design, typography, video editing, and more. With a keen eye for aesthetics and a passion for visual storytelling, Maryam has carved a niche for herself in the dynamic realm of graphic design. Maryam's dedication to her craft is evident in the innovative and captivating designs she produces, making her a notable presence in the vibrant world of graphic design. Always thrilled to collaborate with passionate partners eager to elevate their visual language and push the boundaries of modern creativity.
